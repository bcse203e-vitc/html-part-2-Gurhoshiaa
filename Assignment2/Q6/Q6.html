Classic Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract; beat until smooth.
  4. Gradually add boiling water and mix until fully combined.
  5. Pour batter evenly into prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Let cakes cool completely before frosting and serving.

Did You Know?

Chocolate was once considered so valuable that it was used as currency by the ancient Mayans!

Nutritional Information:

  • Calories per slice: 350
  • Total fat: 15g
  • Protein: 4g
  • Carbohydrates: 50g
  • Fiber: 3g
